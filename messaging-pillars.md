# Messaging Pillars

1. **Private by design** — redaction modes, dual-layer encryption, incognito mode, project isolation, sovereign AI. Proof points: "locked behind your own access code — inaccessible to anyone, including GreenSeek."
2. **Powered by renewables** — renewable-backed infrastructure, cleaner regions, energy-aware deployment as default.
3. **Hyper-efficient intelligence** — adaptive model routing: efficient models for everyday work, deep models only when needed. The eco story is an engineering story.
4. **One regenerative workspace** — Research, Create, Automate (EcoComputer) replaces a dozen tools.
5. **Profits that repair** — the GreenSeek Foundation funds regenerative and earth-repair projects; the 20% impact pledge.

## Audience → lead pillar
- Sustainability-minded professionals & researchers → 2 + 3
- Privacy-sensitive industries (health, legal, finance) → 1
- Teams consolidating AI tools → 4
- Values-driven organizations / B-corps → 5

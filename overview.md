# GreenSeek Overview

GreenSeek is an AI workspace designed for the regenerative future — built for privacy and powered by renewable energy. Its tagline: "Research smarter. Discover cleaner." One workspace to research markets, create deliverables, and run autonomous workflows.

## The three capabilities (EcoComputer)

- **Research** — intelligence workflows: market maps, competitor watches, policy monitors, and custom research agents grounded in live web data and project files.
- **Create** — decks, websites, manuscripts, and exports. Turns ideas into finished, editable, shareable files without jumping between tools.
- **Automate** — scheduled autonomous agents and workflows; a simple creator to compose sources, outputs, and cadence in minutes.

## The framework: Intelligence in Service of Life

GreenSeek is built on Augmented Biological Intelligence (ABI) — technology that serves living minds and ecosystems rather than replacing them. Three commitments:

- **Renewable-powered compute** — renewable-backed infrastructure, cleaner regions, energy-aware deployment by default.
- **Hyper-efficient models** — adaptive model routing: smaller efficient models handle everyday work; deeper models activate only when needed.
- **A Foundation for the planet** — the GreenSeek Foundation directs a portion of profits to regenerative and earth-repair projects worldwide.

## Positioning in one line

Ethical intelligence for a regenerative future — private by design, powered entirely by renewable energy.

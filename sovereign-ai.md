# Sovereign AI

> **Definition:** Sovereign AI means the user — not the platform — controls the data, encryption keys, and privacy rules of their AI experience. In GreenSeek, sovereignty is delivered through dual-layer encryption (content locked behind the user's own access code, inaccessible to anyone including GreenSeek), configurable redaction modes, project isolation, and full data export and deletion.

## Sovereign AI in GreenSeek

1. **Private by design** — redaction modes, dual-layer encryption, incognito mode, project isolation, sovereign AI. ## Frequently asked questions

**How is sovereign AI different from private AI?**

Private AI limits who can see your data; sovereign AI goes further: you hold the keys. With GreenSeek's dual-layer encryption, even the provider cannot access encrypted content.

**Do I need Enterprise for sovereign AI features?**

No. Encryption, redaction, and incognito modes are core platform features; Enterprise adds SSO and custom deployment on top.

# Pricing & Plans

All paid plans start with a 7-day free trial; no charge until the trial ends.

## Starter — $15/month
For professionals and researchers. 1,500 monthly credits, usage-based image generation, 20 projects, 5 GB storage. Chat modes: Eco, Deep, Research, Ultra. EcoComputer workflows (Create, Research, Automations), Workflows, Cloud Tasks, Custom Experts, Code mode. No team workspaces, no priority support.

## Plus — $50/month (Most popular)
For teams and power users. 6,000 monthly credits (includes 1,000 bonus), 100 projects, 20 GB storage. Everything in Starter plus team workspaces, priority support, expedited support, vector search, SSO/SAML.

## Pro (Compute) — $150/month
For compute-heavy workloads and dedicated support. 25,000 monthly credits (includes 10,000 bonus), 100 projects, 50 GB storage, SSO/SAML, priority and expedited support.

## Enterprise — Custom
SSO, custom deployment, org-wide limits, compliance and dedicated infrastructure. Contact sales.

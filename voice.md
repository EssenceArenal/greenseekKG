# GreenSeek Voice

## In one line
Calm confidence, grounded optimism, zero greenwash.

## Principles
- **Grounded, not grandiose.** We say "renewable-backed infrastructure", not "saving the planet with AI". Specifics beat superlatives.
- **Privacy is a promise, not a feature list.** Quote guarantees exactly ("inaccessible to anyone, including GreenSeek"). Never soften or exaggerate them.
- **Regenerative, not just sustainable.** Our frame is repair and flourishing (ABI — intelligence in service of life), not merely "less harm".
- **Efficient by conviction.** Adaptive routing and eco modes are engineering pride, not marketing garnish. Explain how it works.
- **Warm expert.** Write like a knowledgeable colleague: plain verbs, short sentences, no exclamation-mark enthusiasm, no emoji walls.

## Words we use / avoid
Use: regenerative, sovereign, private by design, renewable-powered, adaptive routing, flourishing, repair.
Avoid: revolutionary, game-changing, guilt-framing ("your AI is killing the planet"), unverifiable stats, "military-grade".

## Non-negotiables
- Every eco or privacy claim traces to a garden doc. If it doesn't, mark [VERIFY].
- One CTA per piece.

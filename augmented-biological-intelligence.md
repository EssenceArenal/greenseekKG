# Augmented Biological Intelligence

> **Definition:** Augmented Biological Intelligence (ABI) is GreenSeek's framework for AI that serves living minds and ecosystems rather than replacing them: compute, models, and capital directed toward flourishing — cleaner infrastructure, efficient inference, and repair for the planet.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is ABI a technology or a philosophy?**

Both: a design framework (efficiency, privacy, renewables by default) and a governance commitment (the GreenSeek Foundation funds earth-repair projects).

**How is ABI different from AGI?**

AGI aims to replicate general human intelligence; ABI aims to augment human and ecological wellbeing with intelligence in service of life.

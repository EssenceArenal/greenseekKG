# Audience Profiles

## 1. The Conscious Researcher (Starter, $15)
Independent professionals, academics, consultants. Cares about credible sources, low footprint, and cost. Objection: "is a values-led AI actually good?" Answer with capability first (Research workflows, Ultra mode), values second.

## 2. The Privacy-Bound Team (Plus/Pro, $50–150)
Healthcare, legal, finance teams that cannot paste client data into mainstream AI. Cares about HIPAA-compliant redaction, dual-layer encryption, SSO/SAML, data sovereignty. Lead with pillar 1; be precise, never salesy.

## 3. The Values-Driven Org (Plus/Enterprise)
B-corps, NGOs, climate-sector companies whose procurement includes ESG criteria. Cares about the renewable story, the Foundation, the impact pledge — and needs it verifiable for their own reporting.

## 4. The Tool Consolidator (Plus)
Ops and marketing leads drowning in AI subscriptions. Cares about EcoComputer's Research/Create/Automate breadth and team workspaces. Show workflows, not ideology.

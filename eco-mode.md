# Eco Mode

> **Definition:** Eco Mode is GreenSeek's most energy-efficient response mode, routing everyday questions to compact, renewable-powered models. Alongside Standard, Deep, and Ultra modes, it lets users match compute intensity to task difficulty.

## Eco Mode in GreenSeek

1. **Private by design** — redaction modes, dual-layer encryption, incognito mode, project isolation, sovereign AI. Proof points: "locked behind your own access code — inaccessible to anyone, including GreenSeek." 2. **Powered by renewables** — renewable-backed infrastructure, cleaner regions, energy-aware deployment as default. 3. **Hyper-efficient intelligence** — adaptive model routing: efficient models for everyday work, deep models only when needed. The eco story is an engineering story. 4. **One regenerative workspace** — Research, Create, Automate (EcoComputer) replaces a dozen tools. 5. **Profits that repair** — the GreenSeek Foundation funds regenerative and earth-repair projects; the 20% impact pledge.

## Frequently asked questions

**When should I use Eco vs Ultra mode?**

Eco for everyday drafting, lookups, and quick answers; Ultra for complex reasoning, research synthesis, and long documents.

**Does Eco mode cost fewer credits?**

Lighter modes consume less compute; see your plan's credit details on the pricing page.
